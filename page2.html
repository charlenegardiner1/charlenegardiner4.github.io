You’ll get the freshest produce avaialble, available year round. And a large variety to choose from.

Local sales and farmer’s market create relationship between people and the food they eat, the land on which it is grown and those who grow it. This supportive relationship helps to create an economically stable farm operation. The commuity receive high quality produce often below retail prices. 

Sustainable growing practices benefit the soil, air, water, wild life, and people in the farm ecosystem.

Charlene Gardiner - Farm:  A small, local farm , a bit of brush and marshland that is reserved for the various wildlife native to Abaco. Was started in 2017 with the goal of providing fruits and vegetables that are fresh, nutritious and sustainably grown. We grow a variety of vegatables, fruits and herbs that we sell through local markets. We are a chemical free vegetable farm. Here, we have many gardens,and the beginnings of a new orchard. We are also in the process of building a greenhouse to make available to you a selection of locally grown vegetables year round. We look forward to providing healthy farm fresh produce.
